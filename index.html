Welcome to the Official Eviction Clan Website!

From a Great Community to Great Players, Eviction Clan hosts gamers from every platform who play multiple games. We also host a huge diversity in the clan, from people living in London to NYC. Eviction participates in competitive Esports tournaments monthly. We have Clan Wars against other Fortnite clans regularly. We welcome all sorts of players, from any region. Anyone can join the community, however to join the Competitive Team, you must have a 1.5+ K/D. If you have any questions or inquiries, join our Discord or contact info@evictionclan.xyz for more information. Links are at the bottom. Eviction's vision is "Clan for all, Respect for all."
